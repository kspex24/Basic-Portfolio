About Me

Although, I am originally from New Orleans, Louisiana, I grew up in Brussels, Belgium. Living overseas was a fantastic experience. I really enjoyed meeting people from different countries and experiencing different cultures. I returned to the US for college and attended LSU where I majored in French. After teaching for a short while, I attended paralegal school and worked in the legal department of the EPA. From there, I moved to a small software company called QWIZ, where I worked as a developer on their French language products. Then, I moved back to the legal field working as a paralegal.

After several years of working as a paralegal, I have decided to make a career change. I am currently enrolled in the Full Stack Developer Bootcamp at Georgia Tech. I hope to embark on a new and exciting career in Web Development in the near future.
